jCarousel module:
------------------------
Requires - Drupal 7
         - jCarousel (http://drupal.org/project/jcarousel)
License - GPL (see LICENSE)

Overview:
--------
Provides a jCarousel formatter. Supports Colorbox and Lightbox2 (when 
ported to D7). 


Installation:
------------
1. Download and unpack the jCarousel and jCarousel_formatter modules
   in your modules folder.
3. Go to "Administer" -> "Modules" and enable both modules.


Configuration:
-------------
Go to "Structure" -> "Content types". Choose "Manage display" for the
content type you wish to enable the formatter for. Under "Format" choose
jCarousel.
